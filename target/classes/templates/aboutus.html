ABOUT US

Chiya Sansar offers the finest natural organic tea, hand-picked by local farmers in Nepal's lush Himalayan regions. Their blends are rich in flavor, full of health benefits, and 100% organic.

Committed to sustainability and supporting local communities, Chiya Sansar delivers premium teas that are environmentally friendly and packed with antioxidants, perfect for health-conscious tea lovers.

At Chiya Sansar, we believe in the art of tea. Our dedicated team is passionate about curating an exquisite selection of teas, each with a unique story and character. From classic black and green teas to herbal infusions and exotic blends, we cater to every tea lover's palate.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to share the love of tea with others. What sets us apart is our commitment to quality and sustainability, ensuring that each cup you brew not only tastes great but is also ethically sourced. We invite you to join us on this flavorful adventure and discover what makes Chiya Sansar the perfect choice for tea enthusiasts everywhere.
